Neutral Spine & Pelvis Posture

The neutral spine posture in a healthy spine is an optimal position of three natural curves of the spine at neck region curve, mid back region curve and lower back region curve. Posture is also determined by pelvic tilt with an optimal alignment of Anterior Superior Illiac Spines(ASIS) and Pubic Symphysis along a vertical line running through the leg bones of lumbar and cervical regions and out the top of the head.
